Generator 9.8

Vision of social enterprise
Generator 9.8 is an inclusive and collaborative space. The network of change-makers who inspire positive impact on society through self-expression, education and innovation.


Mission of the social enterprise
Generator 9.8 provides an alternative and affordable co-working space combined with social bar for local and international professionals and creative thinkers from various fields. We offer a participative, supportive working space and platform for personal and professional development.

Community 9.8 shares core values and principles that contribute to common growth and the generation of positive change in the world. It is an inclusive and collaborative space. The network of change makers, who inspire positive impact on society through self-expression, education and innovation.
