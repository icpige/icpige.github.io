Youth Empowerment

In 2015-2016 team of ICPI have provided training programs for the beneficiaries of the  school based youth clubs under the  Tbilisi Urban Development Program  World Vision International in Georgia. According to the agreement several training modules had been created for youth and delivered covering following topics:

  • Effective communication
  • Active citizenship and volunteering
  • Peer Education
  • Drama Therapy
Youngsters were involved in various non-formal education activities with the aim of enhancing and empowering their voices both within their communities and at the national level. Since 2016 based on the memorandum of understanding signed between the International Center for Peace and Integration and the World Vision International in Georgia EVS volunteers hosted by ICPI have been delivering language courses and other interactive workshops for the  relevant groups.
